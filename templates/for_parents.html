Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my child is learning the right concepts?

Every story is aligned with specific Florida B.E.S.T. mathematics standards. Your parent dashboard shows exactly which standards your child has mastered and which ones they're working on.

What if my child is above or below grade level?

You can access stories from any grade level, allowing your child to work at their appropriate mathematical level regardless of their actual grade. Our system tracks progress across all accessed content.

Can multiple children use one account?

Yes! Each subscription supports up to 4 child profiles, with individual progress tracking and personalized recommendations for each child.

How much time should my child spend on stories each day?

We recommend 15-30 minutes per day for younger children and 30-45 minutes for older students. Our content is designed to respect age-appropriate attention spans.
